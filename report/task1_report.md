## 1. Introduction
This report summarizes the analysis of a 5,000-row e-commerce sales dataset.  
The objective is to identify best-selling products, top-performing categories, and key sales trends to support informed business decisions.

---

## 2. Dataset Overview
The dataset includes:  
- **Order Details:** OrderID, OrderDate  
- **Product Information:** ProductName, Brand, Category  
- **Customer Information:** City, State, Region  
- **Sales Metrics:** Price, Quantity, Discount, Revenue  
- **Sales Channels:** Amazon, Flipkart, Meesho, etc.  

---

## 3. Key Performance Metrics

| Metric | Description |
|--------|------------|
| Total Revenue | Sum of revenue from all orders |
| Total Orders | Total number of transactions |
| Average Order Value (AOV) | Revenue ÷ Total Orders |
| Top Categories | Categories contributing highest revenue |
| Top Products | Products generating maximum revenue |

---

## 4. Key Findings
- **Best-Selling Product:** Wireless Earphones generated the highest revenue.  
- **Seasonal Trends:** Sales peak during festival months (October–December).  
- **Impact of Discounts:** Higher discount percentages significantly increase order quantity.  
- **Channel Performance:** Amazon and Flipkart outperform other channels in revenue generation.  

---

## 5. Insights & Recommendations
- Focus marketing and promotional campaigns on top-performing products and categories.  
- Plan seasonal campaigns during festival months to maximize revenue.  
- Utilize discount strategies to drive higher order volumes.  
- Prioritize high-performing sales channels like Amazon and Flipkart.  
- Leverage insights for inventory planning, pricing strategies, and marketing initiatives.
